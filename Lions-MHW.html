A HOLISTIC APPROACH TO COMMUNITY WELLNESS

Our Mission

To reduce the stigma surrounding mental illness and provide accessible, practical resources that support both the mental and physical health of our community.

Alignment with Global Causes

  • Focused on Health (Mental Health, Disease Prevention).
  • Integrating the Eye Clinic for a comprehensive, holistic approach to well-being.

Our Core Goal: To ensure every community member knows that their mental health matters and that Lions are here to help.

OUR SIGNATURE EVENT: WORLD MENTAL HEALTH DAY

The Community Wellness Walk

Objective: Promote physical activity as a powerful tool for improving mental well-being and show community solidarity.

Action: A high-visibility, public walk designed to generate media attention and encourage broad community participation.

Industrial Park Outreach

Objective: Directly address the stress and unique mental health challenges faced by the **busy working population**.

Action: Distributing essential educational materials and contact information for local support services right where they are needed.

Our Goal: A unified display of commitment to health, reaching those who need the conversation most.
